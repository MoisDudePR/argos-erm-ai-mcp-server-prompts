# Are We Exposed Right Now?

## Demo Goal
Quickly answer the most common executive question.

## MCP Context
- Active exposures
- Severity ratings
- Asset criticality

## Prompt
A customer executive asks: “Are we exposed right now?”

Using ERM MCP data:
- Identify any active high or critical exposures
- Explain them in simple terms
- Clarify immediacy and urgency

Provide:
1. Direct answer (yes/no/partially)
2. Brief explanation
3. Recommended next steps

## Expected Output
- Clear and confident language
- No over-alarmism
- Business-aligned messaging
