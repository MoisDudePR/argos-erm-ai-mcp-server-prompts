# Cross-Tenant Risk Comparison

## MSSP Use Case
Compare external risk posture across customers.

## MCP Context
- Risk scores
- Asset counts
- Exposure severity

## Prompt
You are analyzing risk posture across your MSSP customer base.

Using ERM MCP data:
- Compare customers by overall risk
- Identify outliers (high and low risk)
- Highlight common exposure patterns

Provide:
1. Ranked customer comparison
2. Key differentiators
3. Portfolio-wide insights

## Expected Output
- Comparative analysis
- Data-driven conclusions
