# Expansion Opportunity Identification

## CSM Use Case
Identify opportunities for account expansion.

## MCP Context
- Coverage gaps
- Asset growth
- Usage patterns

## Prompt
You are identifying expansion opportunities for a customer.

Using ERM MCP data:
- Identify uncovered assets or business units
- Highlight additional value areas
- Frame opportunities as risk reduction benefits

Provide:
1. Expansion opportunities
2. Value justification
3. Suggested next conversation topics

## Expected Output
- Value-led framing
- Customer-aligned language
