# High-Risk Exposure Identification

## Use Case
Identify and prioritize the most critical external exposures.

## MCP Context
- Vulnerabilities
- Misconfigurations
- Public services
- Exploit intelligence

## Prompt
You are conducting a risk prioritization exercise using ERM MCP data.

Analyze current exposures and:
- Identify issues with the highest likelihood and impact
- Correlate exposures with known exploit activity
- Highlight assets exposed to the internet with critical weaknesses

Provide:
1. Top high-risk findings
2. A brief explanation of why each is critical
3. Suggested remediation priority order

## Expected Output
- Ranked list
- Clear risk justification
- Business impact context
