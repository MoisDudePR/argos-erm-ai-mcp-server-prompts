# Business Impact Analysis

## Use Case
Translate technical exposure into business risk.

## MCP Context
- Asset ownership
- Business services
- Exposure severity
- Threat likelihood

## Prompt
You are briefing non-technical stakeholders on external cyber risk.

Using ERM MCP data:
- Map exposed assets to business functions
- Explain potential operational, financial, or reputational impact
- Identify worst-case and most-likely scenarios

Provide:
1. Business risk summary
2. Impacted business areas
3. Risk mitigation recommendations

## Expected Output
- Business-first language
- Minimal technical detail
